Evaluator

Evaluate the strength of your encrypted data to ensure it meets your security needs. Our Evaluator tool analyzes encrypted text, scores it based on two key criteria, and provides constructive feedback. The first criterion is the complexity of the encrypted data, measured by the variety of unique characters in the ciphertext. The second criterion assesses the similarity between the plaintext and ciphertext. Based on this analysis, you receive detailed feedback that helps you gauge the robustness of your encryption, determining whether it is secure, safe to use, and protected against potential threats.
